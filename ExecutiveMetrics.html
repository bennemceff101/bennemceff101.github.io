Executive Metrics Dashboard

Scenario: We need to provide VPs with a quick and easy layout to view summary statistics for our company.
Where's the Data?: We leveraged OneStream to build and mantain annual budgets. OneStream also will provide reporting on actuals. We have this in Excel via the OneStream connector, but in formats best for creating the company reporting PDFs & PPTs. So why not create a more Tableau Friendly Quick View via the OneStream connector?
Since the customer only really needed the totals, I inserted a text box with the formula =$L$26. L26 was taking the range of cells containing fund names, counts, and amounts and summarizing and formatting with this formula: ="Total Amount: $" & TEXT(SUM(D3:D18),#,##0.00_;-#,##0. ")&CHAR(10)&CHAR(10)&G21&"Total Count :" & TEXT(SUM(C3:C18),"#,##0_;-#,##) ") &CHAR(10)&CHAR(10)&G22 The end product chart is displayed in the After image. It was a quicker, easier solution than multiple charts and gave the customer exactly what they were adding up by hand on a calculator.
*Note that this is all been replaced with dummy fund names and data.
