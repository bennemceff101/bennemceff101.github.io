PowerBI Research Display

I replicated an existing Excel report in PowerBI. In its first version, it didn't differ much from the Excel, but an important addition was an added tab I thought of as a "Research Display". Sure, the customer can right click on a point to "Show Data Point As A Table", but a short detail dashboard view gives a clean view of what we can deliver in early adoption of PowerBI. In Desktop version, I had a "Slicer" tab where FY and Q may be selected by the customer and rolled through all the charts. I used a card visualization on this "Research Display" tab with a measure to display the current FY+Q selection. Then a couple additional slicers for Fund and Division Name just impacted this view.

It just gives the customer a chance to quickly check the most important data items w/o pulling up a comprehensive drill down table. A data table can be interactive, responsive, and relevant.

*Note that this is all been replaced with dummy fund names and data.
